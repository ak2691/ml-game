Machiner Match Components
=========================

Current ruleset
---------------
- duel-v1: two equal fighters; win by reducing opponent HP to 0.
- Timeout: draw.
- Arena: 800 x 800.
- Simulation step: 100 ms.
- Max arena object slots: 5, named object_1 through object_5.

Shared fighter controls
-----------------------
- Movement: move toward target, away from target, strafe left/right, diagonal in/out, cardinal and diagonal directions, move to center, or stop.
- Rotation: face enemy.
- Targets: opponent, opponent grenade, opponent fireball, or object_1 through object_5.

Classes
-------
- Melee
  - Stats: 125 HP, 12 move speed.
  - Swing: 20 damage, 92 px range, 50 degree arc, 1000 ms cooldown, 200 ms active window.
  - Block / shield: 5 max charges, 3000 ms recharge.
  - Dash: 2 max charges, 4500 ms recharge, 1000 ms dash duration, 20 dash speed.

- Ranged
  - Stats: 90 HP, 8 move speed.
  - Fire gun: 10 ammo, 3000 ms reload, 700 px max range, about 1000 ms cooldown.
  - Gun damage falloff: 15 damage at 100 px, 10 at 300 px, 5 at 500 px, 2 at 700 px.
  - Throw grenade: 12000 ms cooldown.

- Mage
  - Stats: 100 HP, 10 move speed.
  - Fireball: 4 max charges, 1000 ms reload, 400 px range, 15 direct damage.
  - Fireball burn: 2 damage per tick, 1000 ms tick, 5000 ms duration.
  - Stun: 10 damage, 184 px range, 100 degree arc, 1500 ms stun duration, 200 ms active window.

Persistent arena objects
------------------------
- Health Pack
  - Type: healthPack.
  - Default size: 42.
  - Effect: heals the first overlapping fighter for 50 HP, capped at that fighter's class max HP.
  - Lifetime: consumed when collected.

- Damage Zone
  - Type: damageZone.
  - Default size: 128.
  - Effect on entry: deals 25 damage when a fighter enters.
  - Ongoing effect: fighter is marked inside the zone; incoming attack damage is multiplied by 1.5 while inside.
  - Lifetime: remains in the arena.

Transient ability objects
-------------------------
- Grenade
  - Created by ranged throw_grenade.
  - Size: 12.
  - Throw speed: 32.
  - Decelerates by 1.6 per tick.
  - Explodes after stopping for 1000 ms.
  - Explosion radius: 50.
  - Explosion damage: 50 at center, falling to 25 at edge.

- Grenade Explosion
  - Short-lived visual/effect object.
  - Visible for 200 ms.
  - Applies grenade damage in its radius.

- Fireball
  - Created by mage shoot_fireball.
  - Size: 30.
  - Speed: 36.
  - Max travel/range: 400 px.
  - On hit: 15 damage and burn application.

Logic brain pieces
------------------
- Logic block: conditions plus one selected action.
- Cluster: grouped logic blocks with shared conditions and priority.
- Priorities: 1 through 10, lower number wins.
- Limits: 50 logic blocks, 12 clusters, 4 conditions per block.
- Conditions cover HP, target distance, edge distance, class cooldown/readiness state, object existence/type, damage-zone status, and expression comparisons.
